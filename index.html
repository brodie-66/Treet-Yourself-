Overview

Treet Yourself Contracting is a YES tree planting company specializing in planting large-scale native trees for farmers and large outdoor spaces. We handle all the hassle of organizing, sourcing, and planting for you. Using cutting-edge technology and AI tools, we bring you the best quality and let you visualize the completed project.

What We Do

Prepping

We visit the site to plan the project, gather data on soil type and wind conditions, and identify the best trees.

Using AI imaging, we show you how the project will look when completed, allowing amendments to fit your vision.

We pre-spray the site to optimize tree survival.

Planting

We organize the trees and protectors, schedule a suitable time, and deploy our crew of qualified planters to get the job done.

Preserving

We follow up after planting to ensure satisfaction and return after six months to monitor tree growth.

Subscribe to a long-term follow-up plan at a fixed rate of $4.00 per tree plus travel costs.
